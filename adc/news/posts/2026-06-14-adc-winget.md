title: ADC will soon be downloadable via Winget
date: 2026-06-14
author: Mealman1551

ADC will soon be downloadable and installable via Microsoft Winget.
Today i opened a pull request on the Winget community repository, adding ADC 1.4.5 into the repo.
It only waits on approval, after approved status ADC is downloadable with:

```powershell
winget install Mealman1551.ADCArchiver
```
