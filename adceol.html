ADC 1.0.0 End Of Life Notice

Please be advised that ADC (ArchivedDataCodec) version 1.0.0 will reach its End Of Life (EOL) on November 15, 2025. After this date, there will be no further updates, bug fixes, or support for this version. I recommend all users to upgrade to the latest version of ADC to ensure continued support and access to new features. The latest version is: 1.4.0 "Grand Canyon" LTS, and can be downloaded from the repository and the project website. Go to the downloads.
