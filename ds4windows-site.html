About DS4Windows

DS4Windows is a tool that allows you to use your DualShock 4 or DualSense 5 controller on a Windows PC. Originally developed by Jays2Kings and later maintained by Ryochan7, the project is now proudly continued by Mealman1551.

With DS4Windows, you can customize your controller settings, map buttons, and enjoy seamless gaming on your PC.
